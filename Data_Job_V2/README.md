## Introduciton

Navigating the Data Job Market can feel like a maze with information scattered everywhere. This Dashboard (V2.0) is particularily designed for **Job Seekers, Job Transistioners & Job Swapper**
to cut through the clutter! Using a real world dataset of 2024 data science job postings- rich with details like titles, salaries, Locations- this project offers streamlined, single-page interface to quickly explore cruical market trends & compensation Insights.

## Dashboard Overview

This second iteration consolidates the dashboard into **single, focused page**, designed to give job seekers the most critical market insights at their fingertips.

![Dashboard Page 1](/images/Project-2_Pg_1.png)
This page acts as your concise mission control for data job. It showcases Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), like **Job Count, Skills Per Job, Median Salaries both Hourly & Yearly**. One can quickly see **Skill Popularity**(by Job Percent or count) & compare **Salaries across different Job Titles**, all designed for efficent overview. 

##  Conclusion

This Updated Dashboard (Version 2.0) showcases PowerBI's ability to transform extensive job posting data into powerful, streamlined tool for career analysis. It empowers **Job Seekers, Job Transitioners, Job Swappers** by allowing them to filter & explore important market insights efficiently in single page, helping them make informed decesions about their next career move.
